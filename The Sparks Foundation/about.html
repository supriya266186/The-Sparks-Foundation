We are a nonprofit on a mission to scale English literacy so that every child has access to the educational and economic opportunities it unlocks. Even in the most remote areas, digital content can now be deployed via low-cost mobile devices “offline,” without requiring continuous Internet connectivity. Our content is designed for seamless scaling worldwide: we do not require prior exposure to English, knowledge of any other language, or supervision by skilled adults. Our immersive digital content is designed for personalized guided learning, presented within an engaging framework that relies heavily on imagery and audio. It is based on instructional second language acquisition research, targeting the fundamental skills required for literacy.

90% of Indian education system is driven by rural Government schools. As a country, we face two chronic challenges in our Indian education system – acute teacher shortage and teaching quality. A grade 5 student’s literacy rate is extremely low, and they are unable to read a grade 2 level textbook. Therefore, with limited access to quality education, we see children between grades 5-8 discontinuing their education, and prioritising earning a livelihood to support their families.

Our intervention is focused on children in grades 5-8, by delivering interactive sessions in the local medium, with the support of a powerful partner ecosystem. All to ensure that children from rural India have access to quality education and continue their education. Our goal is to continue to support these children, and reach out to more, so that no child has to drop out of school.
